Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2T WiredBrainCoffee
  • Marshmellow(optional)
  • Milk(optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into mug.
  2. Hot milk on stove.
  3. Mix milk with cocoa mix in mug.
  4. Sip with ease, Enjoy with ease.
